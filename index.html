About Willow

Willow is an orphan, that was atteact by the shadow creatures from the constant. She escaped thanks to her little friend Teddy, who has become her guardian, ever since the attack. After this experience, Willow has been struggling with her ability to start fires, whenever she was upset. On one dark night, the shaddow creatures came for her once again. Willow and Teddy noticed them to late. In the outburst of anger Willow has started the fire with her abilities and burned down the house, where she has lived alone. This time they succeded and abducted Willow to her new reality in constant.
